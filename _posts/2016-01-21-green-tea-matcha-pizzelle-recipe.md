Green Tea Matcha Pizzelle Recipe

(Makes 10 pizzelle)

- 7 tablespoons + 1 teaspoon all-purpose flour
- 2/3 teasoon baking powder
- 2 tablespoons green tea matcha  powder
- 1 egg + 1 egg white
- 2 tablespoons + 2 teaspoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon honey
- splash of vanilla
