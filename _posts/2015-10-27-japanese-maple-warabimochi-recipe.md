Japanese Maple Warabimochi Recipe 

(makes 3-4 servings)

- 1/3 cup potato starch
- 1 1/4 cups jasmine (or green) tea
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 1 cup Kinako (soybean flour)
- maple syrup for drizzling
